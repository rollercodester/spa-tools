There are many good general-purpose Javascript libraries out there, some modern and some
that are a little long in the tooth.

While **@spa-tools/utilities** is yet another to add to the list, it's succinctly written
in TypeScript with a focus on being requirements-driven. As such, we hope you'll find one
or two or even a few helpers that are just that: _helpful_.
